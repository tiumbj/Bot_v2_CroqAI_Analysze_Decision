4) Contract Authority
==================================================

Authoritative AI contract:
- models/schemas.py :: AIDecision

Meaning:
- Groq output must be parsed/adapted into AIDecision.
- Groq JSON is NOT the authoritative storage contract.
- Parser is responsible for normalization and safe deny fallback.

AIDecision required fields include:
- candidate_id
- decision
- approved
- confidence
- entry_min
- entry_max
- stop_loss
- setup_quality
- trend_alignment
- regime_fit
- exhaustion_risk
- reason
- model_name
- prompt_version
- latency_ms
- valid_response
